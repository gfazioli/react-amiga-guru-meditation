# AmigaErrorBoundary

You may use the `AmigaErrorBoundary` as a ErrorBoundary

## Basic usage

```jsx
<AmigaErrorBoundary>
  ... your components
</AmigaErrorBoundary>
````

Of course, you may use it to display any catchable error in your children components

```jsx
<AmigaErrorBoundary show={true} error={"Hello, I'm error"}>
  ... your components
</AmigaErrorBoundary>
````
